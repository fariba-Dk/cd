About


Technology, New Media, Entertainment and Product Attorney

Christopher Drews is a highly experienced commercial attorney practicing Technology, Entertainment and Consumer Products law in Los Angeles, California as he has for over 25 years. Chris spent nearly 20 of those years with the Walt Disney Company where he served as Deputy Chief Counsel overseeing Business and Legal Affairs for Disney’s interactive, new media, video game and mobile segments.

Chris opened his boutique Santa Monica office in 2020 to assist technology and media enterprises large and small navigate complex commercial transactions, and to serve as outside General Counsel to start-ups and growing companies. Having served on the client side of the profession for most of his career, Chris uniquely understands the legal needs and budgetary pressures facing growing companies. Accordingly, Chris brings a very practical, business-first approach to his legal services by cutting through much of the unnecessary bureaucracy and over-lawyering that frequently burdens complex transactions. The goal is to always deliver superior legal services with practicality and cost efficiency.
